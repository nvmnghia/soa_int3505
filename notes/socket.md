# Socket

## 1. Definition

Socket is a pair `IP:port` [RFC 793]. 2 sockets form a connection.

### 1.1. Types

2 types:

- Stream/Connection-oriented socket: TCP
  - Segment
  - Reliable transfer
  - Flow control
  - Connection management
- Datagram/Connection socket: UDP
